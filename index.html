Carlos Escobero Villarrubia is a multidisciplinary artist, student of Graphic Design at the School of Design of Madrid.

He was born in Parla, Madrid, on February 13, 1996. He studied architecture at ETSAM, where his passion for social and urban fabrics was born. Thanks to this, his vision of design is broad and inclusive, considering this as an engine of change in our society.
